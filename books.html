Books

Sasuke's Story: Sunrise


Naruto's best friend and rival has done a lot of reflecting about his choices and is moving forward with more clarity and a desire to make restitution with his village. Sunrise is a light novel focusing on Sasuke's journey after the end of the series. Readers follow him as he investigates Kaguya and protects the village from the shadows.






Naruto: The Seventh Hokage And The Scarlet Spring


Set between the end of the original Naruto manga and the start of Boruto: Naruto Next Generations, this manga follows Sarada Uchiha, Sasuke and Sakura's daughter, as she tries to find who her real mother is. After finding a picture of Sasuke with another woman, Sarada begins to doubt that Sakura is her birth mother, and journeys to find Sasuke to uncover the truth.






Itachi's Story: Daylight


Readers follow Itachi through childhood to the moment he is accepted as an Anbu, and learn of the dark plan the Uchiha Clan have for Konoha in this light novel. Itachi has always been somewhat of a mystery, with his past shrouded in questions. This novel sheds light on those questions.






Innocent Heart, Demonic Blood


This novel adaptation of the first anime arc is perfect for any fan who enjoyed the intense battles between Team 7, Zabuza, and his subordinate, Haku. This novel is true to the series, and fans will not find much in the way of new material for the arc. However, for those who truly enjoyed this first anime arc, the memories this novel will rekindle may make you fall in love with the series all over again.






Naruto's Story: Family Day

This fun and whimsical light novel follows Naruto and Sasuke, as well as less-seen characters such as Kiba, Shino, and Choji, as they celebrate Family Day. Naruto tries to balance getting a good night's sleep with finding his daughter, Himawari, a Kurama doll that is only sold on Family Day, while Sasuke desperately tries to connect with his daughter, Sarada, leading to him feeling embarrassed more than once.
